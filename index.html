About North Texas Level Up Plumbing

Built On Trust.
Backed By Real Work.

North Texas Level Up Plumbing is a locally owned and operated North Texas plumbing company proudly serving homeowners throughout the DFW area. Built on hard work, honesty, and good old-fashioned Texas values, we deliver dependable, high-quality plumbing work our neighbors can trust.

We understand that plumbing problems don’t just interrupt your day—they affect your home and your peace of mind. That’s why we approach every job, from everyday plumbing repairs to larger plumbing upgrades, with care, craftsmanship, and respect.

Our goal is simple: raise the standard of plumbing in North Texas through solid workmanship, transparent pricing, and a trustworthy, neighbor-you-can-count-on approach.

Repairs

Leaks, clogs, pressure issues.

We handle common plumbing repairs including leaking pipes, pressure problems, clogged drains, and faulty fixtures. Fast diagnostics and dependable repair work done right.

Toilet Rebuilds

Flush issues, rebuild kits.

From weak flushing to running toilets, we rebuild and replace worn internal components. This helps restore performance and prevent wasted water or leaks.

Faucet Repair

Leaks & cartridges.

We repair dripping faucets, damaged handles, worn cartridges, and fixture leaks. The goal is smooth operation, clean water flow, and no wasted water.

Garbage Disposal

Install & replacement.

We install and replace garbage disposals safely and efficiently. We also check drainage, leaks, and proper operation before the job is finished.

Hose Bibs

Outdoor faucet repair.

We repair and replace outdoor hose bibs for leaks, damage, and poor water flow. A proper repair helps protect your exterior plumbing and foundation.

Water Heaters

Repair & install.

From no hot water to replacement installs, we diagnose and service water heater issues. We help keep your home comfortable with reliable hot water.

Drain Cleaning

Clogs & backups.

We clear clogged drains and stubborn backups using professional equipment. This restores proper flow and helps prevent repeat drainage problems.

Gas Leaks

Detection & repair.

We locate and repair residential gas leaks with safety as the priority. Lines, fittings, and connections are checked so the issue is handled correctly.

Camera Inspections

Sewer diagnostics.

Sewer camera inspections help find hidden blockages, damaged lines, and underground plumbing problems. It gives a clearer look before major work starts.
